CPU AMPHITHEATER

This architectural design for an amphitheater combines cutting-edge technology with sustainable design principles:

◈ Inspired by CPU architecture and circuit board layouts
◈ Capacity for 1000+ students with modular RAM-inspired classrooms
◈ Fully solar-powered with integrated battery storage system
◈ Smart thermal management with light-filtering borders
◈ Interactive LED lighting visualizing data flow patterns

The Central Processor Unity (CPU) Amphitheater features a roof design with integrated solar panels that power the entire complex. Special light-filtering borders prevent heat gain while maximizing natural light, reducing energy consumption by 60% compared to traditional structures.

At night, stored solar energy activates LED circuits in the flooring and walls, creating an immersive environment that simulates data flowing through a motherboard. The surrounding modular classrooms are arranged in a motherboard pattern, with pathways representing circuit connections.
